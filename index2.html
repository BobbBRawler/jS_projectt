Want to know about Crazy Cars

The car rental page serves as a platform for customers to browse, compare,
and book vehicles for personal or business use.

How do I book a car online?

You can select your desired car, rental dates, and pickup/drop-off locations,
then proceed to payment to confirm your booking.

Is there a minimum age requirement to rent a car?

Yes, the minimum age is usually 21. Some locations may charge
extra for drivers under 25.

Can I pick up the car at one location and drop it off at another?

Yes absolutely, one-way rentals are typically allowed but may incur an extra fee.

What if I return the car late?

Late returns may result in additional charges.
Check your rental agreement for grace periods.

What happens if the car is damaged?

If you have coverage, damages are usually covered up to the policy limit.
Without coverage, you may be responsible for repair costs.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, Crazy Cars allow changes or cancellations, but fees may apply
depending on the timing, Same car may not be available.
